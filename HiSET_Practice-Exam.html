HiSET essay rubric (scored 1–6)

ScoreDescription
6Well-focused, thoroughly developed argument with strong evidence and varied sentence structure. Virtually free of errors.
5Focused and well-developed with clear evidence. Minor errors that don't interfere with meaning.
4Generally focused with adequate development and relevant evidence. Some errors but meaning is clear.
3Somewhat focused but inconsistent development. Limited evidence. Errors may occasionally obscure meaning.
2Weak focus and development. Little relevant evidence. Frequent errors that interfere with communication.
1Minimal or no focus, undeveloped. Severe errors make it difficult to understand.

Passing = 2 or higher. Two raters score independently; scores are averaged.
